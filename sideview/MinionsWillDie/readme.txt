- Nurse Minion (Idle, Walk, Hit, Die)
- Muscle Minion (Walk, Hit, Die)

- Crosshair
- Title Screen
- Weapons (Pistol, Machine Gun, Rifle, Shotgun, Grenade)
- Font
- Health Pack
- Explosive Barrel
- Barrel
- Bullet
- Laser
- Banana
- Environment (Floor + Wall)
- Random Minion Gore
